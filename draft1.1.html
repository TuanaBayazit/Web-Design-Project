    Volunteer Internship

    Sustainable Project Management Intern
    My first online internship was at 100sCLub on sustainable project management. The fact that March 8 Women's Day was recently gave us the opportunity to design a project that focused on women at the center of this internship. There was no training process during the internship and we were only asked to design an artificial intelligence-integrated project and present it as a group.
    Only the planning phase would be prepared and such a project would be considered as done. Approximately 3 weeks were given for this preparation process. The specific problems I experienced in the group I randomly joined allowed me to improve my tolerance level and emotional state control. Even though we went through a difficult process, we completed the internship in winner place. You can access more information from this link.

    Project Management Intern
    This internship I completed online was about project management. The training process lasted 4 weeks and there were 4 inventories that we had to complete afterwards. There were different trainings every week during the training process and these included the titles "Fundament of Advertising and Marketing", "Fundament of Project Management and Project Management Strategies", "Fundament of New Media and Digital Transformation", "Fundament of Management and Organization". After the trainings that lasted every weekday evening were completed, the inventory files requested by each trainer were delivered to the company on the specified date. In addition to learning new things in a different field, it was also sweet to be given a certificate to those who successfully completed the internship. You can access more info from this link.
